📂 What is this corpus?

This is a Kurdish Kurmanji corpus composed of several literary and linguistic genres:

  • Poetry (Helbest) – Kurdish poems
  • Novels (Roman) – Kurdish novels and stories
  • Theatre (Şano) – Kurdish theatre works
  • Newspaper (Rojname) – Press articles
  • Website (Malper) – Content from Kurdish websites
  • Specific Corpus – Research-specific corpora
  • Traditional Songs (Dengbêj) – Oral traditional Kurdish songs
  • Dictionary (Ferheng) – Kurdish dictionaries and lexicons

🎯 Objectives

  • Preserve the Kurdish language
  • Support linguistic research
  • Advance Kurdish NLP technologies
  • Document the richness of Kurdish literature
  • Archive Dengbêj oral traditions
  • Build cultural and lexicographic resources

🔍 How to use?

  1. Type your word
  2. Select the genre (optional)
  3. Click "Search"
  4. View results in the table

For example, to search in the dictionary:

  • Type the word "ziman"
  • Select "Dictionary"
  • Click "Search"

📖 Dictionary usage

The dictionary section contains words and their meanings. Example:

kurmancî – the most spoken Kurdish dialect

ferheng – a book that contains words and definitions

peyv – the basic unit of language (word)
